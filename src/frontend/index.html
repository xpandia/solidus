The Problem

$4T+ institutional capital sitting on the sidelines.

Banks and financial institutions want to issue stablecoins but lack the compliance infrastructure to do it safely. Without programmable compliance, institutional adoption remains impossible.

No Compliance Layer

Existing stablecoin infrastructure lacks built-in KYC/AML controls required by regulators worldwide.

Fragmented Controls

Freeze, whitelist, and role management require custom smart contracts, increasing risk and cost.

Opaque Reserves

Institutions cannot verify reserve backing in real-time, creating trust gaps that prevent adoption.

The Solution

Three pillars of institutional trust.

Programmable Compliance

Embed KYC/AML rules directly into the token. Every transfer is automatically validated against your compliance framework before execution.

Institutional Controls

Freeze/unfreeze accounts, enforce whitelists, and manage roles with granular precision. Built-in authority separation for enterprise governance.

Real-Time Reserves

On-chain reserve attestation updated every block. Every token is backed 1:1 with cryptographic proof visible to all stakeholders.
