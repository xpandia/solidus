# Solidus Demo Video Storyboard

**StableHacks 2026 | 60-90 Second Demo Video**

---

## Production Notes

- **Total Duration:** 75 seconds (target)
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 minimum, 4K preferred
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps (60fps for screen recordings)
- **Audio:** Voiceover + background music, no webcam unless team intro included
- **Screen Recording Tool:** OBS Studio or Loom (high quality export)
- **Music Volume:** Background music at 15-20% of voiceover volume — present but never competing

---

## Music & Mood

**Overall Tone:** Confident, modern, institutional. Think Apple product reveal meets fintech demo day. Not hype. Not EDM. Clean authority.

**Recommended Music Style:**
- Ambient electronic with subtle build — starts minimal, adds layers, peaks at the transfer hook moment
- Reference tracks: "Experience" by Ludovico Einaudi (piano + electronic), or Spotify search "corporate tech cinematic minimal"
- Royalty-free sources: Artlist.io, Epidemic Sound (search "technology" + "cinematic" + "minimal")

**Mood Progression:**
| Time | Mood |
|------|------|
| 0:00 - 0:15 | Quiet tension. The problem. |
| 0:15 - 0:30 | Clarity. The solution appears. |
| 0:30 - 0:55 | Building momentum. The demo. |
| 0:55 - 1:05 | Peak. The rejected transfer. |
| 1:05 - 1:15 | Resolution. Clean landing. |

**Music:** Single sustained piano note. Quiet. Tension.

"Solidus is the compliance and issuance layer that makes stablecoins institutional-grade. Here's how."

**Music:** Gentle build. A quiet beat enters — not driving, just present.

**Visual:**

5. Quick cut to Solana Explorer showing the new Token-2022 token with transfer hook program attached

**Transition:** Smooth cut. Music intensifies slightly.

**Music:** Build continues. More presence. The audience should feel momentum.

---

### Scene 5: The Transfer Hook — The Peak [0:40 - 1:00]

This is the climax of the video. Every production decision should serve this moment.

**Visual (Part A — Success):**
Screen recording:
1. Transfer panel: 1,000 SUSD from Wallet A to Wallet B
2. Compliance check runs: all green
3. Transfer succeeds — green confirmation banner

Lower-third: "Transfer approved — all compliance checks passed"

**Visual (Part B — Rejection):**
1. Same transfer panel: 1,000 SUSD from Wallet A to Wallet C
2. Compliance check runs: KYC Status — **RED. FAILED.**
3. Transfer is REJECTED. Red banner: "Transfer blocked by on-chain compliance hook"
4. Cut to Solana Explorer showing the failed transaction with the compliance hook error

The rejection should feel sudden and decisive. Consider a subtle sound effect — a clean, low "denied" tone — not dramatic, just definitive.

Lower-third: "Non-compliant transfer rejected on-chain. No human in the loop."

**Voiceover:**
"Step three: operate. When a token moves between verified wallets, the transfer hook validates compliance in real time. Approved."

*[Beat. Brief pause.]*

"But when the same token tries to reach a non-verified wallet — the protocol stops it. On-chain. Automatically. No compliance officer. No API call. No way around it. The rules are in the token."

**Transition:** Quick, clean cut to reserves screen.

**Music:** Peak moment. Full instrumentation for 3 seconds, then pulls back to create space for the closing.

## Editing Notes

### Pacing
- The video should breathe. Do not rush transitions to fit more content.
- Screen recordings should be at 1.2x speed maximum — fast enough to feel efficient, slow enough to follow.
- The pause before the rejected transfer (Scene 5, Part B) is sacred. Do not cut it.

### Text Overlays
- Font: Clean sans-serif (Inter, SF Pro, or Helvetica Neue)
- Color: White text on dark backgrounds, with subtle drop shadow for readability over screen recordings
- Position: Lower third (bottom 20% of frame), left-aligned
- Duration: Each text overlay stays for 3-4 seconds, fades in over 0.5s, fades out over 0.5s

### Screen Recordings
- Record with a clean desktop — no bookmarks bar, no extensions visible, no personal tabs
- Use a custom browser profile with only Solidus-related tabs
- Mouse movements should be smooth and intentional — practice the flow before recording
- If using OBS, set the cursor size to 150% for visibility

### Color Grading
- Slight cool tone (institutional, trustworthy)
- Dashboard should look sharp and high-contrast
- Do not over-saturate — this is fintech, not gaming

### Sound Design
- Voiceover: Record in a quiet room, close to the microphone. Confident, measured pace. Not excited — assured.
- Music: Starts at 15% volume, dips to 10% during voiceover-heavy sections, rises to 25% during transitions and the climax
- Consider a subtle "confirmation" sound effect for successful transactions and a low "denied" tone for the rejected transfer — but only if they sound professional. When in doubt, skip sound effects.

## Alternative Versions

### 60-Second Cut (If Required)
Remove Scene 6 (Reserve Transparency). End with the rejected transfer + closing. The transfer hook IS the product — everything else is supporting evidence.

### 90-Second Cut (Extended)
Add a 10-second team intro before Scene 7. Quick cuts of team members (or their GitHub profiles / contributions). Voiceover: "Built by a team that understands both institutions and infrastructure."

### Silent Version (For Expo Booths)
Replace voiceover with on-screen text overlays. Increase text size. Add captions for every key moment. Music at 40% volume. This version should be fully understandable with no audio.
